What is investing?

Investing is the act of putting your money into assets (like stocks, bonds, or real estate) to grow it over time. The goal is to generate returns and build wealth.

Why invest?

  • Wealth Building: Grow your money over time
  • Beat Inflation: Protect against inflation eroding your purchasing power
  • Achieve Goals: Save for retirement, education, or other long-term goals
  • Diversification: Spread risk by investing in different assets
